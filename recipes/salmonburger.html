Fresh Salmon Burgers with Lemon-Yoghurt Sauce

Description

These are a little like crab cakes, but they're made with fresh salmon and grilled. The secret to juicy (not pasty!) salmon burgers is to chop the salmon coarsely with a knife.

Ingredients

  • 2,5 pounds salmon filet, skin and pin bones removed
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2/3 cup finely chopped parsley
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped green onions
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup plain dried bread crumbs
  • 1 tablespoon seafood seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  1. Chop salmon into 1/8- to 1/4-inch pieces. Transfer to a bowl with 2 eggs, 2/3 cup parsley, 1/4 cup green onions, 2 tablespoons lemon juice, bread crumbs, seafood seasoning, 1/2 tablespoon salt and 1/2 teaspoon pepper.
